About Us

Ubiquitous Medical Services is a dynamic and mobile healthcare provider based in Cape Town. Founded in July 2025, our mission is simple: to deliver reliable, accessible, and professional healthcare wherever it's needed. Our name reflects our purpose — to be present wherever healthcare is required, ensuring that businesses, individuals, and communities have access to essential medical services that promote well-being and safety.
